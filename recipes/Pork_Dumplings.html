Homemade Dumplings

Description

These tasty steamed pork dumplings make a perfect appetizer for a party or you can serve them as a main dish.

Serve with hoisin sauce, hot Chinese-style mustard, and toasted sesame seeds

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • White Sugar
  • Salt
  • Butter or Margarine
  • Milk

Step

  1. Stir together flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Cut in butter until mixture is crumbly.
  3. Drop batter by spoonfuls into boiling stew or soup. Cover and simmer without lifting the lid for 15 minutes. Serve
  4. Serve hot and enjoy!
